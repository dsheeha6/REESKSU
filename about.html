About REES

Who We Are

The Real Estate & Entrepreneurship Society at KSU (REES) is a driven community for students passionate about real estate, business, and investing. Whether you're just getting started or ready to take your next big step, REES provides real-world insight, valuable connections, and the tools to help you grow.

We're committed to doing the best for our members. Meetings include expert speakers—developers, investors, Realtors, and more—market trend discussions, networking events, and practical guidance to help you take action in your career or investment journey.

We're currently working on something big and actively building partnerships to bring even more opportunities to our members.

Join REES and be part of a movement that's shaping the future of real estate and entrepreneurship—together.
