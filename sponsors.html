Our Sponsors

We are proud to partner with organizations that share our vision of empowering students in real estate and entrepreneurship.

Robin and Doug Shore Entrepreneurship and Innovation Center

The Robin and Doug Shore Entrepreneurship and Innovation Center is part of Coles College of Business, The Leven School of Management, Entrepreneurship and Hospitality. Located on both KSU campuses, the Center provides students with a wide range of experience and training opportunities designed to develop their entrepreneurial perspective. Through their support, REES members gain access to valuable resources, mentorship, and opportunities to develop their entrepreneurial skills and bring their ideas to life.

Kennesaw Campus: 3207 Campus Loop Road, Kennesaw, GA 30144

Marietta Campus: 900 Hornet Loop Road, Lower Administration Building (B)
